Quick Setup Guide

  1. Login to Top.gg: Go to top.gg and sign in via Discord.
  2. Find the Cookie: Open DevTools (F12) → Application tab → Cookies → click https://top.gg.
  3. Copy value: Find __Secure-authjs.session-token and copy its entire value.
  4. Paste & Save: Paste it in the input on the left and click Save. Done!
